## Chapter VI. Of the Essential Dignities of the Figures in the Houses; of the Aspects of the Houses; and of the Friendship and Emnity of the Rulers in Aspects, etc.

By Essential Dignity is meant the strength of a figure when found in a particular House. A figure is therefore strongest in what is called its House; very strong in its Exaltation; strong in its Triplicity; very weak in its Fall; weakest of all in its Detriment. A figure is in its Fall when in a House opposite to that of its Exaltation; in is Detriment when opposite to its own House. The following list shows the Essential Dignities; that is to say, they follow the Dignities of their Ruling Planets, considering the twelve Houses of the scheme as answering to the twelve signs, thus: Asc. to Aries, 2 to Taurus, 3 to Gemini, &c., . . . 12 to Pisces. Therefore Mars figures will be strong in Asc. and weak in 7th and so on. See chapter I for attribution of figures to planets.

Caput Draconis is strong in Dignities of Jupiter ♃ and Venus ♀.

Cauda Draconis is strong in Dignities of Saturn ♄ and Mars ♂.

### The Aspects of the Houses

The Asc. is aspected by 11, 10, 9 (as sextile, square and trine) Dexter, and by 3, 4, 5 . . . Sinister, and has 7 in opposition.

The Dexter aspect is that which is *contrary* to the natural order of the Houses; it is stronger than the Sinister. So for other Houses. Figures have Friends and Enemies:—

- Saturn ♄ : Jupiter, Sol, Mercury, Luna Friends; Mars, Venus Enemies.
- Jupiter ♃ : Saturn, Sol, Venus, Mercury, Luna; and Mars.
- Mars ♂ : Venus; and Luna, Saturn, Sol, Mercury.
- Sol ☉ : Jupiter, Sol [*sic*], Venus, Mercury, Luna; and Saturn.
- Venus ♀ : Jupiter, Sol, Mars, Mercury; and Saturn.
- Mercury ☿ : Saturn, Jupiter, Sol, Venus, Luna; and Mars.
- Luna ☽ : Jupiter, Sol, Venus, Mercury; and Saturn and Mars.

Also figures of Fire are sympathetic with those of Fire, friendly with Air and Earth; hostile to Water.

So Water sympathises with Water, friendly Air and Earth, and hostile Fire: Air sympathises with Air, friendly Fire and Water, and hostile Earth. Earth sympathises with Earth, friendly Water and Fire, and hostile Air. Again, sign figures are friends to those sextile or trine, and hostile to those square or in opposition.
