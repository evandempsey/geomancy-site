## Chapter IV. Tables of Witnesses and Judge

The tables are classed by the Left Witness.

The judgement concerning a wife (*e.g.*) will hold good for all demands of the 7th House.

So of the others.

*Editorial notes: (1) Arabic numbers in the cells mean that the judgement is determined by the figure in that House of Heaven. (2) In the printed Puer table the dot-figures of the last two Right Witness columns are transposed; they are given here as the arithmetic of Witnesses and Judge requires, which the cell contents corroborate. (3) Each Judge below has been verified to equal the sum of its Witnesses.*
