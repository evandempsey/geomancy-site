{Leaf 095 (page 67) opens with the book title and prologue, as printed:}

The second Booke of Geomancie.

*The Prologue.*

After that it is sufficiently treated, what thing Geomancie is, and the manner how to practise the same, and what profite and commoditie commeth therof by the subtiltie of the Science, and the receation which is taken therein, together with the knowledge & experience which the Geomancian ought to have in Astrologie: now resteth it to open and discover the qualitie of this Art by the xij. houses of heaven, and their significations which be as followeth.

Of all the significations being of the first house, upon the questions and demaundes which may be propounded and made in the same. Chap. I.

The first house which is, Thoroscope or Ascendant and Angle of the Orient, contayneth the signification of the demaundes which may be moved upon the qualitie, complexion, and the length of the life of man, that is to say:

1 In what disposition is the person, the intent of his will, and the place of his abiding or dwelling.

2 The beginning of things which presently he will take in hand and execute, and the longnesse or shortnesse of his life, and the qualitie of his heart.

3 Any thing that he beareth hidden uppon him, and the alteration of his hearts desire.

4 The making, Phisnomy and proportion of his bodie, to knowe if he be or shall be well made or ill, and whether he be faire or hard or ill favoured.

5 The nativitie of the person, whether he shall be healthy, or sickly: strong, or weake.

6 The beginning of all enterprises.

7 The joy and sadnesse of the person.

8 As touching the members of man, & other living things, this house contayneth the questions which may be propounded and made of the head, and of every things therein contayned: as the braine, memorie, understanding, reason, intelligence, eyes, eye-browes, nose, teeth, mouth, eares, and visage: wherefore when ye finde a figure in the first house, whatsoever it be you shall judge after the maner and fourme that we will shew hereafter, and give alwayes the first house unto that that the question which is made or propounded doth note to, that is to say, the motive of the querant.

## Aquisitio — house 1

When ye finde this figure in the first house (called Aquisitio) ye shall say that it signifieth goodnesse, happinesse, and quiet life, with the accomplishment of the persons owne harts desire. If the demaund be of or for voyage, it signifieth good successe thereof, but he that taketh it in hand, shall not depart so soone as he thinketh: but yet shall he returne home in all gladnesse, with the accomplishment of his desire. And if the question be made for love and in way of marriage, it is good, and for any thing that a lover doth demaunde in that behalfe: for Aquisitio is a figure of the Ayre and of ♃ D in the signe of ♈ and the exaltation of ☉ a figure firme entring in good for all thinges but for warre: but in Church causes and voyages she is merveilous good.

## Amissio — house 1

When ye finde in this first house the figure called Amissio, you shall say that it signifieth losse of inheritages and of possessions, an ill life, an ill beginning and ill issue of any enterprise, ill for sickenes, voyages, and quicke dispatching: to be briefe, this figure is ill in all demaundes that can be propounded, except for prisoners, in which it signifieth deliverance and escape out of prison: in all demaundes of women, it signifieth whores, and ill weomen, for this figure is a figure of ♀ ♄ in the signe of ♏ and of the element of the fire.

## Fortuna major — house 1

If in the first house you chaunce upon this figure called Fortuna major, it signifieth good will, good heart, loyaltie, prosperitie in all things, joyfulnesse and riches, in cause of a woman it betokeneth joyfulnesse, amitie of Kings, great Princes and Lordes: this figure is good in all things (except to keepe a thing secret, in which thing it is a token of small suertie. This is a figure of ☉ D in the signe of ♒ a figure of the Earth fixed entring in.

## Fortuna minor — house 1

In finding this figure called Fortuna minor, at the beginning it betokeneth cholar, hastinesse, and swiftnesse in al things, it is good for the affayres of warre, and signifieth force & boldnesse of heart, with victorie over enemies, it is good also for voyages, in other things it is not so good: as touching thinges of love it signifieth a contentment of the thing pretended, but the matter shall be disclosed. This figure Fortuna minor, is a figure of ☉ ♄ in the signe of ♉ and of the element of the fire.

## Leticia — house 1

Finding in the sayd house this figure named Leticia, it doth signifie a peaceable and quiet life, conjunction and amitie of merry and pleasant persons, honest, of good heart and will in all things. This figure is good except in matters of warre. In case of love it signifieth some lying and dissembling, that is the person will promise much and perfourme but litle: this is also a figure of ♃ ♄ in the signe of ♉ and of the element of the Ayre.

## Tristitia — house 1

If that by chaunce this figure called Tristitia, be in the first house, it is a signe of an ill beginning of the enterprise. The man is of ill will, and pensive, a traytor and disobedient. This figure is ill in all things, but to serch treasure in the earth, she is also good in matters of fortifications and buildings: this is a figure of ♄ R in the signe of ♏ and of the Element of the Earth.

## Puella — house 1

When ye finde this present figure named Puella, it doth signifie joy, to singe & daunce, to play, to be well clothed and neat, to be in love, a man of good will, young and a lover of gardens: she is good in case of love, and in all things, but in warre, this is a figure of ♀ D in the signe of ♎ and of the Element of Water.

## Puer — house 1

Finding in this house this present figure called Puer, it is a token of joy, and to make feastes and bankets with Ladies it also betokeneth theft, robberie, deceite and gaine in play: in matter of love it is good, and likewise in warre. For voyage it betokeneth a meetely swiftnesse, for marriage it is good ynough signifying alwayes great deceipts and fraudes. It is indifferent in all thinges, but better for warre than for any other thing. This is a figure of ♂ in the signe of ♈ and of the element of the Ayre, wherefore it is deceptive, subtill and wittie.

## Rubeus — house 1

All those (almost) which in olde time wrote of Geomancie, as the Indians, Caldians, and Hebrewes, would that this figure called Rubeus, when that it is founde in the first house, should be judged like unto the other without rejecting it, and say that it doth betoken, craft, fraud, subtiltie, treason, warre, debate, discorde, battell, spilling and shedding of bloud. And to be briefe, it doth betoken all the wickednesse in the world. But the Egyptians and a great part of the Latines, say that in framing a figure, this figure fortune to be in the first house, that it should not be judged. But that figure must be broken and make an other, because that in this place this figure is nothing worth, which thing the Author of this boke hath often times seene and knowen by experience, and that is the cause that I am of the oppinion with the Egyptians and Latines, to breake the figure when that it is found in the first house. And so at this time I will speake no more thereof.

## Albus — house 1

If in the first house ye finde this figure called Albus, it doth signifie to have gaine by travell in white things, and that a person is sound, pleasant, fine, merry, and happy in all things. If the demaunde be for sute of lawe, it is a signe that the man shall winne. If it be for message he shall have good newes, in case Mercuriall it is very good because ☿ hath his joy in the first house. It is also good for marriage, but it is nought for warre, and good for peace, this is a figure of ☿ D in ♋ and of the element of the Water.

## Conjunctio — house 1

When ye finde this figure called Conjunctio, in the first house for, that he is a figure of ☿ R in ♍ which is his exaltation it is very good in all kinde of Mercuriall things, it is also good in every other thing, so that she be accompanyed with good figures, and she is ill if she be founde with the ill figures, she alwayes signifieth, alliance, concord, and conjunction with a friend, or woman great with childe. And this figure is of the Element of the Ayre.

## Caput draconis — house 1

Finding this figure called Caput draconis, that is to say, the greater beare, it signifyeth goodnesse in all thinges, except for warre, for it signifyeth combate and battells, and for peace it is perfectly good. It is also good for marriage, but it will be long in doing, it is good in matter of gaine: to be briefe, it is good for all thinges that ye can demaund, and signifyeth health of bodie, pleasant and merry, and to have the favour of kinges, Princes, and great Lordes with prosperitie and obteyning of their desire. And for that it is a figure of ♃ and ♀ according to the opinion of some men: but by mine opinion of ♃ and ☿ in the signe of ♍ and of the element of the Earth, and for that cause is good to builde houses and to labour the earth.

## Cauda Draconis — house 1

This figure called Cauda Draconis, or the lesser beare, found in the first house, it is like unto the figure Rubeus, whereof we have spoken before: and therefore the fygure ought not to be judged when it is found in the first house, but must be broken and an other to be made, neverthelesse there be some that holde the opinion that the judgement should not herein be delayde, for what cause soever the demaunde was asked & the fygure made: but it signifyeth evilnesse, losse and damage for all thinges that may be asked of: so that it is good for nothing but for ruynes, and burninges of Countryes by warres and treasons, by reason that it is a figure of ♂ and ♄ in ♐ and of the element of the Fire.

## Carcer — house 1

When you finde this fygure called Carcer in this fyrst house, it signifyeth ill opinions and thoughts melancholique, heavines, sorrowes, envies, angers, great paine and travaile: the person to be a Saturnian, mellancholy and dreaming of malice, it is ill in all thinges, but to make rampiers, forts, and other fortifications of Townes, and to defende them well for which it is good to take a journey in hande, or for voyages it is ill: for it betokeneth staying, and that the person or his horse shall be hurt by the way on the feete. This is a figure of ♄ D in ♓ and of the element of the Earth, and therefore is an ill figure.

## Via — house 1

This figure called Via, found in the first house, signifyeth a staying in the way, and small health in the journey: it is ill in all thinges except it be to goe out of prison, for the which it is good, and because it is a figure of ☽ in her decrease in ♌ and of the element of the Water, and for that I doe not finde it good, I will say no more at this time.

## Populus — house 1

This figure named Populus, found in this house is alwaies good in all things as well in warre as in peace: signifying alwaies a company of people gathered together for one thing or other, in case of marriage it is perfectly good, and likewise to take a journey in hand signifying swiftnesse, and more by water then by land, and because it is also a figure of ☽ in her full in ♑ and of the element of the Water, it therefore betokeneth raine and that the person which is on the way shall be moyled.
