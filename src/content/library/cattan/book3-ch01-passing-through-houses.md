The third Booke.

A briefe deduction of the accord and signification which the sixteene figures have by the twelve houses.

CHAP. 1.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Acquisitio: two points, one point, two points, one point" data-figure="acquisitio"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Acquisitio</span></span></span> IF this figure named *Aquisitio*, do in mounting growe from the first house into the seconde, that is to say, that he be in the second or be like unto the first where *Aquisitio* is, it signifyeth to gaine gownes, and clothing apparell, to get honour, and to make gaine and profit in all acts of honour and vertue, and with the like people.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If that from the first she goe into the third, it signifyeth to have honour, joy, and profite of the kinsefolks and friends.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If that shee goe from the first to the fourth, it signifyeth profite by the father, and a good and joyfull end of his affaires.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If that shee goe from the first to the fyft, it signifyeth joy of children, that is to say, they shall be good, and of honour, and to have joy and pleasure, to eate and drinke, and to bee well clothed.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> If that she goe from the first to the sixt, it signifyeth profite and good lucke for servants, and to buy small cattell.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 7</span> If she goe into the seventh, it signifyeth to make a contract of enemies, to make marriages, and to get honour and the love of persons, and for all demaundes it is good.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 8</span> If she goe from the first into the eight, it signifyeth death for the thing demaunded, or for the demaunder and great profite in succession and heritages : it is also good for thinges of magicke.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 9</span> If she goe from the first to the ninth, it signifyeth to have profite and gaine by voyages, and in things of the Church, as in benefices or offices : it is also good for the profite of the children that yee would put to schole, for they shall have promotions Ecclesiasticall, and they shall be men of knowledge.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 10</span> If she passe from the fyrst to the tenth house, it signifyeth amitie, familiaritie, acquaintance and profite with kings, princes, and great lordes: likewise to receive honour and dignitie by the mother : and also profite in succession and heritages. And to be briefe, it is good in all things.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 11</span> If she goe from the fyrst to the eleventh, it signifyeth to have honour and profite by his friendes, and a good hope of the question demaunded.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 12</span> If she passe from the fyrst to the twelfth, it signifyeth imprisonment, losse of beastes and to be overcome by the enemie: and to be briefe, it is ill in all thinges.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Amissio: one point, two points, one point, two points" data-figure="amissio"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Amissio</span></span></span> If you fynde this fygure *Amissio* in the fyrst house, and from thence be found in the seconde house, which we call the going from one house unto an other, it signifyeth losse of goods, and to fall into debates, quarrelles and contentions and sute for goods.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If she passe into the third house, it signifyeth anger and contentions betweene kinsfolks and allyes, it is also ill for small and short journeyes.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If she goe into the fourth house, it signifyeth anger betweene the children and the father, and betweene the father and the children, and by that meanes losse of heritages, and the beginning and sute in lawe the one against the other : and this copulation and assembly of the fygure is very ill but for lecherie.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If she passe into the fift house, the woman with childe shall have unseasonable deliverance : it is also ill to eate and drinke, and signifyeth anger about victuals.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> If she passe into the sixt it signifyeth anger against servants, and losse of them and of small cattell.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 7</span> If she passe thence into the seventh, it signifyeth strifes, quarels and debates with friendes : it is also ill for marriages, and signifyeth to have displeasure with his wife, and to lose the sute.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 8</span> If she goe into the eight, it signifyeth mortalitie by hoat fevers, and alteration of the understanding, losse of goods of the women, and of the enemie : and in all the demaundes it is ill.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 9</span> If she passe into the ninth, it signifyeth losse of substance, and to be robbed by the way : it is ill for substance of the Church, and for men learned.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 10</span> If she goe into the tenth, it signifyeth anger and miscontentment of kings, princes and lordes, and of the sea, and it is ill in all the demaundes of the tenth house.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 11</span> If shee passe into the eleventh, it signifyeth anger against his friend, and to have an ill issue of the thing pretented or hoped to have.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 12</span> If she goe into the twelfth, it signifyeth losse of cattell, and imprisonment. And to be short, it is ill in all things.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Fortuna Major: two points, two points, one point, one point" data-figure="fortuna-major"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Fortuna Major</span></span></span> If this figure *Fortuna maior* be in the first house, and thence leape into the second, it signifyeth a great gaine in goods, moveables, as gold, silver, and rich utensilles.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If she passe into the third house, it signifyeth to have much substance by the meanes of the kindred : it is also good for small and short voyages.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If it passe into the fourth, it signifyeth the father to be of good and godly disposition: it is also good to buy heritages and possessions, and a good issue in all things.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If shee goe into the fift, it signifyeth joy, pleasure, to be in good order, well appointed and clothed, to eate and drinke well, and to have fewe children, and they shall be of red colour.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> If it passe into the sixt, it signifyeth fidelitie in servantes, and that the sicke person shall amende: it is also good for small beastes.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 7</span> If shee passe into the seventh it is good for marriage, and signifyeth that the enemie will make peace : for the acquaintance of a Ladie it is not good, because their secretes shall bee disclosed.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 8</span> If it goe into the eight, it signifyeth death : it is ill in all things but to have the goods of a woman.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 9</span> If shee passe into the ninth, it signifyeth to contract an amitie with Church men, and men learned, to make a voyage it is good.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 10</span> If it goe into the tenth, it signifyeth to have dignitie with kings and princes, and with the mother, and to have victorie over his enemies.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 11</span> If shee passe into the eleventh it signifyeth a good issue upon a good hope, good friendes and succorable.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 12</span> If it goe into the twelfth it signifyeth imprisonment of the Prince, and that his enemies shall over passe him in might and power.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Fortuna Minor: one point, one point, two points, two points" data-figure="fortuna-minor"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Fortuna Minor</span></span></span> If this figure *Fortuna minor*, be in the first house, & thence goe into the second, which we call going from one house to an other, it signifyeth a meane in gaine, and he which medleth with red things shall quickly lose thereby.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If she passe into the third, it signifyeth joy of the kinsfolkes, it is also good for a short voyage, and signifyeth that it shall bee quickly ended, it is also good for friendes.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If it passe into the fourth, it signifyeth that the father is angry or sicke, it also signifyeth the losse of the sute.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If she come into the fift, it signifyeth to have many children which shall be red of colour, also great joy and pleasure, to eate and drinke, and to be costly and well apparelled.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> And if she go into the sixt, it signifyeth good to buy small cattle, it is indifferent for servants, and signifyeth that they shall be wittie and wise.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 7</span> If it passe into the seventh, it signifyeth marriage, and that it shall be with strife and contention, the enemie is strong and mightie, the woman is wise, it is ill in case of love, for it shall be discovered.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 8</span> If shee goe into the eight, it signifyeth death, and also is ill in all things of esprite, as Magicke and invocations.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 9</span> If it passe into the ninth, it signifyeth long voyages with all prosperitie and good lucke, and the amitie of men of the Church.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 10</span> If shee goe into the tenth, it is good and prosperous going to the service of a king, Prince or great Lord, the shippe on the Sea shall come safe and sounde, and the owner thereof shall shortly have newes.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 11</span> If it passe into the eleventh, it signifieth good and trustie friendes, and a good end of the thing wherein hope is put.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 12</span> If shee goe into the twelfth, it is ill in all things but to buy horses.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Rubeus: two points, one point, two points, two points" data-figure="rubeus"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Rubeus</span></span></span> If you finde *Rubeus* in the first house, I will say no other thing but that which before I have tolde you, that is to say, that following the opinion of all the Doctors in this science, be they Caldeans, Indians, Hebrewes, Arabyes, Egyptians or Persians, when this figure is founde in this place, it ought not to be judged, the which thing I have alwayes founde true by long experience, wherefore at this time I will say no other thing : but that she is ill in all the houses but in the sixt.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Albus: two points, two points, one point, two points" data-figure="albus"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Albus</span></span></span> If you finde this fygure *Albus* in the fyrst house, and be found againe in the second house, it signifyeth gaine and profite in white things, writings or letters, or bookes.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If she passe into the third, it signifyeth good time for friendes and kinsfolkes, and that letters shall come from neare at hand.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If it goe into the fourth, it signifyeth to winne his sute, it is also good in the demaundes which may be made upon the father, signifying as well in them as in all others a good issue and ende.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If she passe into the fift, it signifyeth to have many children, and to delight in white clothes, to eate and drinke, and to company often times with learned men.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> If it passe into the sixt the servants be good and trustie.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 7</span> If she goe into the seventh the marriage lately begunne shall take effect to the great contentment and profite of the parties, and the enemies shall demaund peace.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 8</span> And if it passe into the eight, it signifyeth death by a hoat disease of rumes and cathers, it is also good for magicke.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 9</span> If she goe into the ninth, it signifyeth that the long journey shalbe good and profitable, and that the letters which come from a farre off bring good newes, it is also good for Doctors & churchmen, and to make amitie and acquaintance with them.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 10</span> If it passe into the tenth it is good to goe to Kings, Princes and Lordes, and to goe to visite his mother.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 11</span> If shee goe into the eleventh it bringeth good lucke, and also for letters which shall come on their part.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 12</span> If it passe into the twelfth, it is good to buye frames, also in this place it is a token to be held prisoner.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Caput Draconis: two points, one point, one point, one point" data-figure="caput-draconis"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Caput Draconis</span></span></span> If this fygure *Caput draconis* be in the first house, & thence ascende into the seconde, it signifyeth gayne and profite in all things.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If it passe into the third, it signifyeth the kinsfolkes to be of good amitie, it is also good for short voyages, but that there shall be some slownesse therein.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If she goe into the fourth it signifyeth good for the father, and to succeede in his heritage.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If it passe into the fift it sygnifyeth many children which shal be wise and wittie, it sheweth also a heartinesse to eate and drinke.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> If she goe into the sixt it signifyeth gaine and profite in buying of cattell, and that the servants be good and loyall.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 7</span> If it passe into the seventh it is a good time for marriage and for reconciliation of enemyes, and that the person is of great amitie.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 8</span> If shee goe into the eight it signifyeth death without remedie, and sometime in this place it signifyeth to winne inheritaunces.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 9</span> If it passe into the ninth, it signifyeth gaine and profite by a long voyage by men of the Church.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 10</span> If shee goe into the tenth it is good to goe to Kings, Princes and to the mother.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 11</span> If it passe into the eleventh it signifyeth to have good friends, and that the hope shall not be in vaine : and also the thing that is demaunded shall come to a good effect.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 12</span> If she goe into the twelfth, it sygnifyeth to be made prisoner, and therein to have much vexation and torment, and in all the demaundes she is ill, but for to buy horses.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Cauda Draconis: one point, one point, one point, two points" data-figure="cauda-draconis"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Cauda Draconis</span></span></span> If this figure *Cauda draconis* be found in the first house, then the figure should not be judged, but it must be broken and an other made one houre after that : but if from the seconde house she goe into any of the other ye may there judge it, saving for the fourth, where there is no certaine judgement to be given, for the malice of the sayde fygure and therefore at this time I will say no more.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 2<span class="source-margin__sep">; </span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Laetitia: one point, two points, two points, two points" data-figure="laetitia"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Laetitia</span></span></span> Finding this fygure *Læticia* in the first house, and thence go into the seconde, it signifyeth a mediocritie of gaine by white thinges, and in thinges of the Church.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 3</span> If it passe into the third, it signifyeth the kinsfolkes to be merry and in good disposition and in health of body, it is also good for small and short voyages.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 4</span> If she goe into the fourth, the end of all thinges shall be joyfull, but the promise false.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 5</span> If it passe into the fift, it is good for children, and to eate and drinke, and to sing musicke, and signifyeth a great lightsomnesse of heart.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span>1. 6</span> If she goe into the sixt, it is good for servauntes, signifying that they shall be profitable to their masters: in all the other houses this fygure is good, but in the eight and twelfth, for in the eight she signifyeth death, and in the twelfth losse of cattell. And for because that according to the signification of ech house I have herebefore sufficiently written : you may by the same easely judge ech question that is demaunded, I will not hold you long in the other fygures which followe in touching their mutations and concordances, but onely passe over them generally.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Tristitia: two points, two points, two points, one point" data-figure="tristitia"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Tristitia</span></span></span> This fygure *Tristitia* found in the first, and thence goe into the second, it signifyeth small profite but in things of the earth, as vines, medowes, woodes and lands earrable, in all the other houses this figure is ill, but in the fourth and eight where both in the one and the other, it is good to buy heritages, & because I have largely declared here before, I will nowe say nothing more at this time.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Puella: one point, two points, one point, one point" data-figure="puella"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Puella</span></span></span> If this fygure *Puella* goe from the first house to the second, it signifyeth gaine by women, and by white things, in all the other houses where this figure passeth shee is good, especially in the house where she signifyeth joyfulnesse : but in the eight she signifyeth death, and in the twelfth imprisonment.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Puer: one point, one point, two points, one point" data-figure="puer"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Puer</span></span></span> If this figure *Puer*, passeth from the first to the second, it signifyeth small gaine but in things of warre, in all other houses this figure is meane, but in the eight and twelfth, she is ill.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Conjunctio: two points, one point, one point, two points" data-figure="conjunctio"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Conjunctio</span></span></span> This figure *Conjunctio* going from the first to the second, it signifyeth gaine by bookes and writings, in all other houses this figure is indifferent, but in the seventh where it is good for marriage, and in the ninth to put schollers to studie, and in the eight it is ill, for it alwayes signifyeth death, and in the twelfth it is neither good nor badde.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Carcer: one point, two points, two points, one point" data-figure="carcer"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Carcer</span></span></span> This figure *Carcer* passing from the first into the second it is gainefull to buy landes earrable, in all the other houses where ye finde her, ye shall judge her as ye doe the others, that is to say, according to the signification of the house, where shee is, and therefore at this time I will speake no more thereof.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Populus: two points, two points, two points, two points" data-figure="populus"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="2"><i></i><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Populus</span></span></span> This figure *Populus* passing fro[m] the first house to the second, it signifyeth gaine and profite in white things, and in landes which lye by the water, and if she passe into the third, it is good to make voyage by water, it signifyeth death in the eight and in the ninth, and in the twelfth to be taken prisoner. In all the other houses judge according to their significations.

<span class="source-margin"><span class="source-margin__label">margin</span><span class="library-glyph" role="img" aria-label="Via: one point, one point, one point, one point" data-figure="via"><span class="library-glyph__dots" aria-hidden="true"><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span><span class="library-glyph__line" data-points="1"><i></i></span></span><span class="library-glyph__name">Via</span></span></span> This figure *Via* going from the first to the second, it signifyeth small gaine, if it passe into the third it is good for short voyages : in all the other houses this figure is ill, but in the ninth and tenth, where she is very good, in the eight shee signifyeth death, and prisonment in the twelfth.

When so ever ye finde the first houses to passe one into an other, you shall judge according to the signification of the house where they passe, as I have in the beginning tolde you at large, and now in the end more briefely, the which things to the ende ye may the better understande them, I will set you an example of a figure which my Lord of Tays commaunded me to make, to knowe whether the French king Francis the first of that name, and the Emperour Charles of Austriche, the fift of that name, should speake together: which figure being made, and by fortune *Aquisitio* being in the first house, went straight into the seventh, which is the house of kings and Emperours which was the cause that I straight way judged that the Emperour should speake with the king, and so likewise shall yee judge of the second if it passe into the third, to knowe (as by way of example) if the demaund be made for substance it signifyeth that the substance shall come into the handes of the kinsfolkes of him which made the question according to the content of the demaund, and the signifycation of the house where the sayde figure is : if the second passe into the third, fift or sixt, or into any of the others following, you shall also judge according to the signifycation of the house where it is : the like shall ye do by the third if it passe into the fourth, or into any of the other, unto the twelfth, so shall ye doe by the fourth if she passe into the fift, or into any of the other houses following: and so al the others judging as is aforesaide, according to the signifycations of the houses where they goe, and according to the good or ill of the sayde figures. And ye must note that they never passe but one time to stay the judgement, although that a figure formed have many of one sorte, and all a like, whereof I will speake more at large hereafter in declaring the example that I will set.
