1 This twelfth house called the Cadant from the angle of the South, otherwise called the evill Spirite, comprehendeth naturally the signification of the Questions and demaundes which may be propounded upon a prison, and of the darkenesse thereof, and also of the prisoner therein detained.

2 Also upon the desolation of a person, and of his lamentations and mournings.

3 Also uppon an incureable sickenesse, as the leprosie, the gowte, the paulsie, the dropsie, and griefe of the eies, and such like.

4 Upon the questions and demaundes which may be made upon a traitour, ill servantes, and théefe of an house, and uppon sinne, and the place where it was committed and done.

5 Also uppon a privie ennemy rather then upon an open.

6 And upon great beasts, as oxen, cammels, dromodaries, elephants, lions, beares, wolves, leopards, harts, dragons, serpents, horses, mules, asses, and all other beastes that beare and be ridden upon.

7 Whether the prisoner shall parte out of prison, and when.

8 Whether he shall be racked, if he say trueth.

9 If the partie shall be laide in prison, whether he shall bée sicke there.

10 If it be good to buy great beasts, as oxe, or horse, and the like.

11 Whether the horse shall be good and quicke uppon the hurre.

12 Also if the person be able to pay his debts.

13 Whether he shall be pore hereafter.

14 And if there be any traitours in the house, of what condition and estate they be.

15 Whether a person shall be banished from his Country, or otherwise.

16 Whether a man may boldely goe before his ennemy to unhorse him without any daunger, and whether a man shal be afraide of his ennemies, and of those which doe aide them.

## Aquisitio — house 12

When ye finde this figure *Aquisitio* in this twelfth house, it signifieth that the prisoner shall not come out of prison, and he shall be sicke, and being examined shall confesse the trueth : the person shall be put in prison : it is not good to bestowe any thing in great beasts : the horse is neither quicke nor swift, and hath a disease in his head : the horse or other thing lost will not returne againe : the man will not pay his debtes, he shall bée pore : there are no traitors in the house : the person shalbe banished and driven out of the Countrey : if a man goe to encounter his ennemy he shall have the worst. In all the demaundes which ye may make of the things touching this house this fygure is ill, for it signifieth paine and travell without profite, and that such travell shall returne to losse with great hinderance and povertie.

## Amissio — house 12

This figure *Amissio* in this house, the prisoner shall not come out of prison, but shal be sicke, and confesse a trueth being examined, the man shall be made prisoner : it is not good to buy horses, but if any be bought they shall be swifte, the horse lost shall not be found againe : the person shall pay his debts, notwithstanding he shall be pore : there be traitours in the house : the man shall be condemned, whipped, and tormented, he shall be banished out of his Country : it is not good to goe in the face of the ennemy to harme him, for if he doe he shall be the loser, for the ennemy shall have great aide. This figure is ill in all the demaundes of this house.

## Fortuna maior — house 12

Finding this figure *Fortuna major* in this house the prisoner shall be delivered from prison, and shall not be sicke therein : the person shall have no feare : the ennemy shall be overcome : by travell there shall be much gaine : the prisoner shall not be in question, the person shall not be put in prison : it is good to buy horses, the horse shall be good and runne well, the horse lost will returne againe : the person shall pay his debts, he shall be rich, he shall not be punished, he shal not be chased out of his Country : it is good to goe to set upon the ennemy, for he shall gaine much thereby : the ennemy shall have no aide or succour. This Figure is good for anie Question propounded in this house.

## Fortuna minor — house 12

This figure *Fortuna minor* in this twelfth house, the prisoner shall escape out of prison : the person shall have much losse in méeting with his ennemy, the person shall not be made prisoner : it is good to buy beasts, for there shall be profite in selling them againe, the horse shall be good, swift, and nimble, the horse amongest other things lost shall returne by himselfe : the man shall be rich and not troubled, the man shall pay his debtes, the man shall not be banished out of his Countrey : the enemy shall not have succour or aide of anie person. This figure is good in all the demaundes of this house, but to obtaine maistership or to attend upon an other it is not good.

## Letitia — house 12

Finding this figure *Letitia* in this house, the prisoner shall not be long in prison : it is not good to assault the ennemy, for thereby he shall have more losse then profite : the person shal not be prisoner : it is very good to buy beasts, for therein shall bée much gaine : to hyre a servant, and to aire the ground it is verie good : the horse ye would buy is very good of foote : the man shall pay his debts, the person shall be rich : the ennemy shall have no aide. There is nothing for which ye may make demaunde in this house but this figure is good therein.

## Tristitia — house 12

This figure *Tristitia* in this house signifieth a great number of ennemies mightie and strong : there shall be great heavinesse for the losse of a servant, and of beastes, and for long kéeping in prison and torment thereof. This figure is very ill for all the demaundes of this house saving for Nigremancie and finding of treasure.

## Puella — house 12

Finding this figure *Puella* in this twelfth house, it signifieth profite and commoditie by travell, losse for enemies, because they have offended God : the horse which ye would buy shall be faire, it is good to buy cattell. This figure is good in all the demaundes which ye may demaund in this house.

## Puer — house 12

Finding this figure *Puer* in this house it signifieth anger and indignation against great Princes and Lordes, and against men of war without any occasion, also anger against his owne friend : the prisoner shal be manacled in prison, but he shal come forth after that, if the eight and sixt houses do consent : it is good to buy a horse : it is good to goe in the face of the enemy. This figure is very good for any thing which ye may demaunde in this house.

## Rubeus — house 12

This figure *Rubeus* in this house signifieth povertie and desolation in strange Countries, with fewe enemies, the person shall have much paine and small profite in all his doings. This figure prognosticateth an ill issue of all the demaundes of this house.

## Albus — house 12

This figure *Albus* in this house signifieth shorte deliverance out of prison : it is not good to meddle with the ennemie : the person shall not be prisoner : it is very good to buy great beasts, for thereof will arise much gaine : it is good to labour the earth, and to buy heretages, and to hyre a servant : the horse which ye would buy shall be good and swift, the horse or other thing lost will not returne againe : the man can not pay his debtes : it is good to goe to vanquish and overcome the enemy: the man shall be rich and shall not be tormented. In all the thinges which yée may demaunde in this house this figure is good.

## Coniunctio — house 12

Finding this figure *Conjunctio* in this house, the companies be ill : the thing lost will be founde againe : to buy beastes it is meane, and so is it to buy landes and heretages, and to labour the earth. This figure is meane in all the demaundes of this house, but for melancholinesse, because of companies.

## Caput draconis — house 12

Finding this figure *Caput draconis* in this house, signifieth deliverance of the prisoner, and good time to goe to deface the ennemy : the person ye thinke for shal not goe to prison : it is good to till the earth, buy heretages and cattell, for therof shal gaine ensue : it is good to retaine a servant : the horse which ye would buy is good and runneth well, the horse or other thing strayed away will be found : the man shall pay his debtes, he shall bée rich without any trouble : the enemy shall have no aide. In all the things which you can demaunde in this house this figure is good, except for fire workes, for the which it is nothing good.

## Cauda draconis — house 12

Finding this figure *Cauda draconis* in this house, signifyeth to holde a prisoner : it is not good to goe to meddle wyth the enemy : he that we doubt of shall be put into prison : it is worth nothing either to buy cattell, labour the earth, or to retaine servants : the horse that ye woulde buy is not good, but heavy in his limmes : the thing lost will not be found againe : the man hath no minde to pay his debtes, he is in daunger to be pore, and to have much adoe in his life time, the ennemy shall bée succoured. For all the demaundes which ye may make in this house this figure is ill.

## Carcer — house 12

Finding this figure *Carcer* in this house, it is but méetely fortune for the prisoner, or to him which would assault his enemy to overcome him : the person shall be taken prisoner, but he shall not bide long therein : touching buying of beasts, and tilling of earth, and in all other demaundes in this house this figure hath a meane.

## Populus — house 12

This figure *Populus* in this house, the prisoner hath many enemies which will séeke his death, or have him to be perpetual prisoner : meddle not with the enemy, for he will either slay or take you prisoner : it is good to buy great beasts and horses: the man can not pay his debtes : he is in danger to receive displeasure of his creditours by his imprisonment. For all other questions and demaundes which ye may make in this house this figure is ill, and signifieth alwayes assemblies of people, to doe some harme or vexation.

## Via — house 12

Finding this figure *Via* in this house, it signifieth deliverance out of prison, without incurring ill or displeasure : it is good to goe to assault the enemie : it is good to buy cattell and heretages. Yea, and in all other things which ye may demand in this house this figure is singular good.
