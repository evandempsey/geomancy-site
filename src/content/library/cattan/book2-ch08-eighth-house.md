<div class="source-chapter-heading"><p class="source-chapter-heading__label">Chap. 8.</p><p class="source-chapter-heading__title">Of the eight house and of his demaundes.</p></div>

1 The eight house called the succedant of the Angle of the Occident, otherwise called the house of death, containeth naturally the questions and demaundes which may be made and propounded uppon the sicknes or death of any man, if he shall live long or die quickely.

2 Also to knowe whether he shall die within a day, month, or yeare.

3 If a person shall die a good death, or else be slaine.

4 Whether shall die first, the father or brother of the querant.

5 If a man be slaine who killed him.

6 Who shall inherite.

7 This house containeth the demaundes which may be made touching the goods and substance of a friend, and uppon the treatie of the marriage of a woman.

8 Who is the secrete Counsailour of your enemy, or of your wife, and whether shee doe keepe faithfull companie or not.

9 Also the demaund which may be made uppon fright or feare of harme to come, as by fyre, burning, or shedding of bloud.

10 Also how the person doth that is from home.

11 The gaine or profite to be doone in a straunge Countrey.

12 When he that is from hence shall returne, in moneth, day, or yeare, and what profite he hath doone whilest he was from home.

13 If the feare ye be in shall come to good or bad.

14 Whether the person unto whome ye have given any thing to keepe will restore it againe or not.

15 Whether he that hath given his money to usurie shall gaine thereby.

16 After what sorte shall the good or harme come to you that you shall have.

17 Finally, this house doth containe the questions which may be made uppon the experience of Nigromancie, invocations of Spirites, Inchauntementes, and other divellish Artes.

18 As touching the partes of mans body, it containeth the questions which may be made concerning the privy and secrete members of man and woman, and therefore when ye fynde any of the fygures following, ye shall judge according as yee shall hereafter fynde.

## Acquisitio — House 8

When in this eight house ye fynde this Figure Aquisitio, it sheweth the death of the patient before it be long, the man shall be the heire of the dead man, he shall die a faire death in his bed, the brother shall die before the father: the woman is verie rich: that man hath not killed him whome the question is uppon, the woman hath good and honest company: he which counsaileth the enemy is a grave and wise man, so that if he give counsaile to the wife you neede not to feare any harme or inconvenience: the things shall be neither scortched or burned, there shall no bloud be spilt, he that is from home is in health, and shall returne with much silver: it is ill for all things of Nigromancie and divellish Artes: in all things this Figure is good, but for the sicke person, for he shall die.

## Amissio — House 8

Finding this Figure Amissio, it signifyeth the death of the sicke person, if the first and tenth agree unto this: the sickenesse shall not be very long, the man shall loose the succession and have no amendes for the trespas: the man is in the daunger to die on the wheele or some other evill death: the father shall die before the brother, if the fourth and tenth consent: the woman or lemman is not very rich, the partie that is suspected hath slaine a man about a womans cause, the woman hath ill company, he which counsaileth thy enemy and thy wife, is an ill man, and giveth no good counsaile, the feare is nothing: for the experiences of Nigromancie it is ill (except it be for love, and for that it is not very good, for that love will not continue) the man in the strange Countrey shall lose all, and shall bring nothing home, and for to say the trueth, this Figure is evill in all things.

## Fortuna Major — House 8

When ye find Fortuna maior in this house, the sicke person shall not die at this time, so that the fourth agree with this house, but the sickenes shall be something long, if the tenth doe give consent: the man shall inherite, the man shall die no evill death, the father shall survive the brother, the woman is rich enough, and so is the lemman: the counsailer of the enemy and of the wife is a good man and a secrete, the man needeth not to be afraide: this Figure is ill for the experience of Nigromancie and Arte divellish: in all things which ye may demaund in this house this Figure is good.

## Fortuna Minor — House 8

When in this house ye fynde Fortuna minor, sometime it signifyeth the death of some great lord, and yet he shall amend, according as the first, sixt, or tenth houses doe consent: the sickenes shall not long endure, the man shall have much adoe about his inheritance, the party suspected for the death of the man hath slaine him indeede: the woman is meetely rich, it is an ill man which counsaileth the enemy and thy wife, the man needeth not to feare any thing at all: it is ill for Nigromancie and diabolicall Artes: in all things which you may demand in this house this Figure is meetely.

## Laetitia — House 8

Finding this Figure Leticia in this house the patient shall not die of this disease, the man shall be some mans heire, the woman is meetely rich. And to be briefe, this Figure is good in all the demaundes which ye may make (saving for Nigromancie and Arte diabolicall) for the which it is not good, but for to flee in the aire, or to goe on one side or other.

## Tristitia — House 8

When that Tristitia is in this house, the man is still in a great fright, the man shall inherite by the death of an other. In all other things whereof question may be made in this house, this Figure is evill, but for Nigromancie, for the which it is good: the man shall not be slaine, but shall die some faire death.

## Puella — House 8

When ye fynde Puella in this house, the sicke person is in danger to die, the enemy is in great fright, the woman and leman be rich enough, the sickenes shal not be long, the man shal inherite, the partie hath slaine no man, the father shall live long: in all things which ye may demaunde in this house this Figure is good, except in Nigromancie.

## Puer — House 8

This Figure Puer in this house, the man shall be slaine, it is ill for the sicke person, for it signifieth death if the others consent thereunto: the sickenes shall not be long, the counsailer of the enemy and of the wife is ill, and counsaileth them to doe ill, the woman is not very rich: in all the demaundes which ye propound in this house this figure is ill, saving for love, and for Nigromancie, whereunto it is good.

## Rubeus — House 8

Finding this Figure Rubeus in this eight house, the man is or shall be slaine wyth a sworde or a staffe, the sicke person is in danger to die if the other houses doe consent: the enemy and the wife have ill counsaile, the sicknes will be shorte, the man is in great feare, the woman is not rich: touching Nigromancie and the Arte Diabolicall, it is neither good neither sure, because the man is too hasty in his doings.

## Albus — House 8

When this Figure Albus is in this house, the man shall die in the water, the sicke person shall not heale or amend, so that the others consent thereunto: the disease commeth of a rheume or waterishnes, the woman is rich, he that counsaileth the enemy and the wife is honest, and counsaileth nothing but good, the man needeth not to be in feare, the person hath slaine no man, the father shall live long: in all the demaundes of this house this fygure is good, except for Nigromancie and Diabolicall Arte, in the which it is ill, unlesse it be to make men sing and dance.

## Conjunctio — House 8

If in this house ye finde Coniunctio the sicke person shal die of this disease, the man in prison shall die by the lawe, the man shall not inherite but lose his sute, the man is in great feare, the counsailer of the enemy and of the woman is ill, the father shall quickely die, the man from home shall not bring home much silver: in all the demaundes of this house this Figure is ill, except for Nigromancie and invocations of evill spirites, for the which it is good.

## Caput Draconis — House 8

This Figure Caput draconis in this house, the person sicke shall be long sicke, but he shall have no other disease, the prisoner shall be quite delivered, but not without great expences & cost: the man shall have his inheritance and overcome the partie: the man is without feare, the counsellour is an honest man, the father shall live long, he that is from home shall profit in his voyage: for all the demaundes which may be propounded in this house this figure is good.

## Cauda Draconis — House 8

Finding in this house Cauda draconis the person shall be long sicke and in daunger of death, the prisoner is in daunger of his life, the man shall not have the patrimonie that he looketh for, the man is in a marvelous feare: he that is suspected for the murther hath committed the fact, the counsell given to the wife is neither good nor honest, the man shal make no great gaine in the voyage: in all demaunds this figure bringeth no great good lucke, but rather to the contrarie, but for Nigromancie it is good.

## Carcer — House 8

This figure Carcer in this house, the patient shall be more like to die then to live, the prisoner shall die in prison: it is for succession of heretages good, but not without great paine and travaile, it also is a token of fright and feare. The murther was committed by one which laie in waite to doe it, and did it in some out or darke place, and he that is accused did it, he that counselleth the wife is not of the clearest in the worlde: he that is in trafficke shall doe his businesse well enough, but he is in danger to be robbed by the way: in all the questions which may be propounded in this house this Figure is evill (except it be for Nigromancie) for the which it is marvellous good.

## Populus — House 8

Finding this figure Populus in this house the sickenes will be long, and the patient in danger of death, but yet in long time he shall amend (so that the first and tenth doe consent) the man shall die in his bed by a rheume, or else be drowned in water: the man is much afraid of men of armes, he that is accused of the murther hath done the fact, and used to be much in his company: the counsellour of the wife and of the enimie is good, the man from home shall not bring much substance home with him: in all things this figure is meetely good (saving for Nigromancie) for the which it is very ill, unlesse it be to make a river to come into a place.

## Via — House 8

This figure Via in this house, the sicke person shall die on this disease, if the first and tenth consent thereunto: the man shal not inherite: the counsellour of the wife and the ennimy is indifferent, the man in trafficke shall bring nothing home, the wife and the lemman be very poore, the man hath slaine no body, and if he be in prison he shall come forth to his praise: in all the questions which ye may propound in this house this figure is evill, but for Nigromancie, to cause one to be carried away a farre off.
