This transcription treats the left figure as the First Witness and the right figure as the Second Witness. The printed Judges were checked against the line-parity sum of the Witnesses; the known exception is noted in its entry.

- dots: w1=1,2,2,2 w2=2,2,2,1 judge=2,2,2,2
- uncertain: true
> Note: Printed inconsistency. The second witness as printed reads 2,2,2,1 (Tristitia) — a duplicate of column 3 — but the printed Judge clearly reads 2,2,2,2 (Populus). Witnesses as printed would yield Carcer, not Populus. By the completeness rule (the eight odd figures once each across the leaf) the intended column is Laetitia + Laetitia → Populus; the second witness appears to be a compositor's error. Dots above are AS PRINTED; the judge is as printed and matches the intended pairing.
