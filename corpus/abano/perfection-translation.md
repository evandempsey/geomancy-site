### On the Translation of Figures

Translation is when a figure carries the disposition from one significator to another. For example: someone asked whether he could contract a marriage through his go-between (translator); and such a question fell out: there was Acquisitio in the house of the querent and a three-line scribal pattern in his conjunction, making a good translation to the seventh, which joined it to him in the eighth house, making the disposition of marriage; and because the ascendant was Acquisitio and fortunate, and because the conjunction Coniunctio was procreated from the first and seventh, and because the aspect of the fifth house strengthened the ascendant: nevertheless, because of the multiple succession of the same, he contracted marriage easily.
