The Asc. is aspected by 11, 10, 9 (as sextile, square and trine) Dexter, and by 3, 4, 5 . . . Sinister, and has 7 in opposition. The Dexter aspect is that which is *contrary* to the natural order of the Houses; it is stronger than the Sinister.

Also figures of Fire are sympathetic with those of Fire, friendly with Air and Earth; hostile to Water. So Water sympathises with Water, friendly Air and Earth, and hostile Fire: Air sympathises with Air, friendly Fire and Water, and hostile Earth. Earth sympathises with Earth, friendly Water and Fire, and hostile Air. Again, sign figures are friends to those sextile or trine, and hostile to those square or in opposition.
