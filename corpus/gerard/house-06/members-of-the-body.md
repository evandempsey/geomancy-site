Because *Aries* hath the head, *Taurus* the neck, *Cancer* the breast and lungs, *Leo* the heart and stomach, *Virgo* the belly and intestines, *Libra,* the reins & loins, *Scorpio* the secret members, *Sagittary* the thighs, *Capricorn* the knees, *Aquary* the legs, and *Pisces* the feet.
