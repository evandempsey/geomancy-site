Afterwards in the second house, let that sign be placed which immediately succeeds the other. In the third house, the third sign, and so place the rest in order until you come unto the end of the signs; and make one square figure divided into twelve equal parts, and therein place the signs in order, as it is in Astrology, & as you may find them in this figure: neither are we here to regard the witnesses, or Judge, or any other thing which belongs to Geomancy; but only the sixteen figures, that by them we may have the twelve signs, to which they agree; and observe the manner of the figure as it is here placed.
