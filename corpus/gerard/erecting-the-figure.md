It is expedient therefore to make four unequal lines, by the points casually set down; and to join together those points; and out of the points which are not joined together, which do remain in the heads of the lines, (as it is done in Geomancy) extract one figure; and the sign of the *Zodiack* that answereth to the figure, put for the Ascendant, for the words sake.
