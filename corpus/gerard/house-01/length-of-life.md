IF you are desirous to know concerning the life of any man whether it be long or short, behold the Lord of the Ascendant, who if he be in strong Angles, it signifies long life; in succedents, a middle age; and in cadent houses, a short life; and if he be in strong Angles, he signifies greater years; if in Succedents, meaner years; if in Cadents, lesser years.
