Afterwards it is requisite to make four lines by course for every Planet, by points casually pricked down; and likewise for the *Dragons Head,* as you have done for the Ascendant, and divide those points by twelve, and that which remaineth above twelve, or the twelfth itself, if a greater number doth not remain, retain, and the Planet for which the projection was made, place in that house of which the superabounding number shall be; that is, if there remain twelve, let the Planet be placed in the twelfth house; if ten, in the tenth house; if one, in the first house; if two, in the second house; and so of the rest. And you ought always to begin from the *Sun,* and afterwards from the *Moon,* then from *Venus* and *Mercury,* and from *Saturn, Jupiter* and *Mars,* and the *Dragons Head* and *Dragons Tail*.
