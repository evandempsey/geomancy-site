[figure: 1,1,1,1]

When you find this Figure called *Via*, in the first house, it signifieth a staying in the way, and small health in the journey; it is ill in all things except it be to go out of prison, for which it is good, because it is a Figure of *Hasmodai* the twenty sixth day of the Moneth, and of the Element of the Water.
