When in the first house ye find this Figure called *Puella*, it doth signifie Joy, to sing and dance, to play, to be well cloathed and neat; to be in Love, a man of good will, young, and a lover of gardens She is good in case of Love, and in all things but in War. This makes a party voluptuous and given to vain pleasures, yet gives a good constitution of body, a long and healthful life, few or no diseases that are hurtful, a lover of all kind of delights, as ornaments of the body, society, musick, odoriferous things, love-toyes, &c. of elegant and good manners, given to pleasant studies, much beloved of women: it is *Kedemels* Figure, and her *Idea Zuriel*.
