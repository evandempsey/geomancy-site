When this figure *Fortuna Major* is in the first house it signifieth good will, good heart, loyalty, prosperity in all things, Joyfulness and Riches, in case of a woman it betokeneth Joyfulness amity of Kings Princes and great Lords: This figure is good in all things (except to keep a thing secret in which thing it is a token of small surety, Because it is a figure of *Sorath*, and his Idea *Mechiell* in this place) It is also of the fire fixed entring in, And therefore it signifieth a high mighty man given to command, coveting to bring men into subjection to him, it is good for war and signifieth victory over Enemies, having power sufficient to accomplish his designs, neither can his enemies prevail against him, though they be never so eminent but will rather do themselves injury then him, its in most things good and shews a Heroick and Magnificent sprited Man.
