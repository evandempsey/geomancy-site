This Figure *Populus* passing from the first house to the second, it signifieth gain and profit in white things, and in lands which lye by the water, and if she pass into the third, it is good to make Voyage by water, it signifieth death in the eighth and in the ninth, and in the twelfth to be taken prisoner. In all the other houses judge according to their significations.
