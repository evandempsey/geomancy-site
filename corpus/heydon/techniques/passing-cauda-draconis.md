If this Figure *Cauda draconis* be found in the first house, then the figure should not be judged, but it must be broken and an other made one hour after that: but if from the second house she go into any of the other, ye may there judge it, saving in the fourth, where there is no certain judgment to be given, for the malice of the said Figure, and therefore at this time I will say no more.
