If you find *Rubeus* in the first house, I will say no other thing but that which before I have told you, that is to say, that following the opinion of all the Doctors in this science, be they Caldeans, Indians, Hebrews, Arabies, Egyptians, or Persians, when this Figure is found in this place, it ought not to be judged, the which thing I have always found true by long experience, wherefore at this time I will say no other thing, but that she is ill in all the houses but in the sixth.
