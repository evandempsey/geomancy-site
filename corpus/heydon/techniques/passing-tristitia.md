This Figure *Tristitia* found in the first, and thence go into the second, it signifieth small profit but in things of the earth, as Vines, Medows, Woods, and Lands aerable, in all the other houses this Figure is ill, but in the fourth and eighth, where both in the one and the other, it is good to buy heritages, and because I have largely declared herebefore, I will say nothing more at this time.
