In the eighth house, it denotes sudden death without any long sickness or anguish, and oftentimes sheweth death by the Water, giveth no inheritance, possession or legacie from the dead; and if any be, they shall be lost by some intervening contention, or other discord, he signifies the dowry of a wife to be little or none.
