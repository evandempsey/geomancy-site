In the second house, she sheweth a mean substance, and that to be gotten with great difficulty: maketh a man also always sensible of laborious toyl, things stolen are never regained, what is lost shall never be wholly recovered, that which is hidden shall not be found. But if the Question be of a Thief, it declareth him not yet to be fled away, but to lye lurking within the City.
