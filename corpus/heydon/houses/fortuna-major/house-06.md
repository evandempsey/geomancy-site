In the sixth house, he freeth from diseases, sheweth those that have infirmities shall in a short time recover; signifieth a Physitian to be faithful and honest to administer good Physick, of which there ought to be had no suspicion; household servants and Ministers to be faithful; and of animals he signifies horses.
