In the second house it signifies poverty, and maketh thieves and robbers, and such persons as shall acquire and seek after their maintenance and livelyhoods by using false, wicked, and evil, and unlawful Arts, preserveth theeves, and concealeth theft, and signifies no treasure to be hid nor found.
