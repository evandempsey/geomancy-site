In the ninth house it signifies very few journeys, and those that do apply themselves to travel, their journeys either are about the Messages and Embassages of Princes, or Pilgrimages to fulfil holy vows: sheweth a man to be of a good Religion, of indifferent knowledge, and who easily apprehendeth all things with natural ingenuity.
