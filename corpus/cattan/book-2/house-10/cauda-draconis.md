Finding this figure Cauda Draconis in this house, it signifyeth losse of the Realme, Dominion and Principalitie : for this figure in that matter is very ill. I will say no other thing at this time, but that in all questions and demaunds it signifyeth death, treason and losse of goods : and to tell you at two wordes, this figure is very ill but for warre, for it signifyeth treason, but to make fire or fire-worke and worke of Alkamy, it is good.
