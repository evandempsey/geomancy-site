This figure Populus in this house, there is a great number of people assembled by the commaundement of the king or Lorde about some wedding, or some agreement of peace : it signifieth some time a great number of men of warre assembled: it is good for honour, and dignitie, and for the Physitian, and to take medecine : the mother weepeth : the friend is good : the othe is not good : the king or Lord will never give any thing except it be thinges of waters or rivers : the king loveth not the man : the king shall not tarry long in the Realme : he that shall be chosen Pope, King or Emperour, is of a noble house, the man hath not his health : the man shall be put from the Court, but he shall quickly returne againe : if the king goe to warre he shall have the victorie : the king will doe justice: it is good to take a voyage by water, for he shall safely returne with speede : it is so-so for the ayre, for it will raine often times. In all things this figure keepeth a meane, but for things touching the water, or to make marriages, for the which it is very good.
