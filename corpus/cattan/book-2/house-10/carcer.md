This figure Carcer in this house, the person shall obtaine honour and prayse of the Prince or Lord, by meanes of a woman: sometime it signifyeth detention and imprisonment of a King, and his great miscontentment : the house that he is deteyned in is old and broken : it signifyeth losse of goods : it is good for the Physitian, and to take medecine : the thing lost will never bee found againe : the mother is melancholy : the friend is not trustie : the king will give nothing : the oth is false : the sicke person shall die : the king shall live long, but it shall be in great disquiet : the partie shall not be Pope, Emperour or King, as hee pretendeth : the man shall not be put from the Court, but hee shall continue there to his great miscontentation : it is not good for the king to goe to warre, for he shall be taken : the king will doe good justice : it is not good to take a voyage, for the way is ill : the person farre away will not come quickely : the ayre is not good or holesome. In all the other demaundes which ye may demaunde in this house, this figure is not good, saving for treasure hidden in the ground, and for nigromancy, for the which it is good.
