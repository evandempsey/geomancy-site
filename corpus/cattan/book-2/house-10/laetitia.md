Finding this figure Leticia in this house, the King or Lorde is not sicke : it is good for honour : the Physitian is a good man: and it is good to take and use of his advise : it is good for the mother, Uncle and Aunt : the King or Lorde loveth well the servant, whereby he shall have profite : the Lorde shall have dominion and governance over the landes that hee pretendeth to have : the King or Lord is not sicke : the person shall not be driven out of the Court : the king shall not goe to warre, but there shall be peace: the King or Lord shall doe good justice with loyalty : the King shall make the voyage in great joy and blisse: it signifyeth faire time, cleare ayre, sweete and pleasant, without raine or boysterousnesse. In all thinges which yee can demaunde in this house this figure is good, but signifyeth rather warre then peace.
