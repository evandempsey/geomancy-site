Finding this Figure Leticia in this house the patient shall not die of this disease, the man shall be some mans heire, the woman is meetely rich. And to be briefe, this Figure is good in all the demaundes which ye may make (saving for Nigromancie and Arte diabolicall) for the which it is not good, but for to flee in the aire, or to goe on one side or other.
