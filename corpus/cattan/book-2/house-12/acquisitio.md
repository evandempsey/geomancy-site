When ye finde this figure *Aquisitio* in this twelfth house, it signifieth that the prisoner shall not come out of prison, and he shall be sicke, and being examined shall confesse the trueth : the person shall be put in prison : it is not good to bestowe any thing in great beasts : the horse is neither quicke nor swift, and hath a disease in his head : the horse or other thing lost will not returne againe : the man will not pay his debtes, he shall bée pore : there are no traitors in the house : the person shalbe banished and driven out of the Countrey : if a man goe to encounter his ennemy he shall have the worst. In all the demaundes which ye may make of the things touching this house this fygure is ill, for it signifieth paine and travell without profite, and that such travell shall returne to losse with great hinderance and povertie.
