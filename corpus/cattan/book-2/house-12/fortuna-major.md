Finding this figure *Fortuna major* in this house the prisoner shall be delivered from prison, and shall not be sicke therein : the person shall have no feare : the ennemy shall be overcome : by travell there shall be much gaine : the prisoner shall not be in question, the person shall not be put in prison : it is good to buy horses, the horse shall be good and runne well, the horse lost will returne againe : the person shall pay his debts, he shall be rich, he shall not be punished, he shal not be chased out of his Country : it is good to goe to set upon the ennemy, for he shall gaine much thereby : the ennemy shall have no aide or succour. This Figure is good for anie Question propounded in this house.
