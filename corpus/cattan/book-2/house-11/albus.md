Finding this fygure *Albus* in this house, it is good fortune for the querant : the friend is good and trustie, and will aide you to his power, and so will the companion : the entraunce of the king shall be very good : the mother and king have store of money, and shall not be robbed : the promise shall be kepte : there shall be no dearth, the fortune of the yeare shall be very good. In all the questions which you may make in this house this figure is good, and especially for a friend, and to send children to goe to schole, and to hyre servants.
