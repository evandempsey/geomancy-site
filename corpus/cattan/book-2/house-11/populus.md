Finding this figure *Populus* in this house, it signifyeth many friendes : the fortune of the querant shall be good, and the issue of the thing demaunded : the friend and companion be good and faithfull : the entrance of the king shall be good, the promise meane, the mother and the king have much mony : the fortune of the yeare is good, the yeare plentifull of all thinges, and good cheape. In all things demaunded in this house this figure is good.
