When ye finde this figure *Via* in this house, it signifyeth joy among friendes, and that the fortune of the querant shall be good : the thing desired shall take effect, the promise shal be good and kept, the friend and companion be faithfull and good : there be letters on the way : the entrance of the king shall be joyfull. This figure is good in all things which ye can demaund in this house.
