This figure *Cauda draconis* in this house signifyeth ill fortune for the querant, the friend is naught, the fortune of the thing demaunded shall have an ill issue : the promise shall not be kept : the companion is not secrete : the entraunce of the king shall not be good, the kings and mothers money shall be stolen: the fortune of the yeare shall be ill, a deare yeare. There is nothing contained in this house but this figure is ill for it, saving for the love of Ladies, and fire workes, for the which it is very good.
