This figure *Amissio* in this house, there is no faithfulnes in the friend, but all is dissimulation, and will not ayde you, but rather doe you harme, and doe more displeasure then an enemie: he that yée put in trust will doe the like : the fortune of the querant shall be ill, and so shall his hope be also : the mother and the king have no money : the yeare shall be scarse, and the fortune of the yeare ill. In all demaundes which yée may demaunde in this house, this figure is ill, but for the hope and love of Courtesans.
