This figure *Tristitia* in this house is ill fortune to the querant : the friend is ill, a quareller, and a man of an ill minde toward his friend, the promise shal take none effect, the hope shal be in vaine : the companion is no wise man : the yeare will bée barren, and victuals deare, the fortune of the yeare ill. This figure is ill in all the demaundes of this house, but for invocation of wicked spirites and things of Nigromancie.
