If by chance ye finde this figure Puella in this house, the enemie hath no minde to worke displeasure unto the other: the sute shall have good successe, the person is not robbed: the fugitive will come home againe, it is good for a woman, a leaman, marriage, and play: there will be no warres, but firme peace: the married woman and the leaman haunte others then becommeth them. If that a figure be made to know whether the friend doe love faithfully, this figure signifyeth that the love is fervent: the woman or leman gone away will be found in the end: the person is of a good esprite and hath understanding in musicke and to play on instrumentes, the thing lost will be found againe, the agreement shall be made and last long: in all the demaundes contayned in this house this figure is good but for warre: by this figure it signifieth that the woman that is married is with childe by others then by her husband.
