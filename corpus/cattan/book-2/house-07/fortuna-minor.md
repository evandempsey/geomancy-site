If in this house ye finde this fygure Fortuna minor, it signifyeth that the enemie is wicked and of an evill heart and affection, strong & mightie, and entendeth much harme: the plaintive shall winne his sute, but not without great paine, travaile and diligent soliciting: the theefe is subtill and craftie: the fugitive will not be found, neither returne againe: the woman is choloricke: the marriage will not be for the small profite that commeth thereof: the man is ill minded: the gamester shall lose or have but small winning: the warres will be great: the woman hath to doe with more then her husband: the mayde is no virgine: the woman or friend lost will not returne againe: the woman is not of the richest: the man shall goe to warre & have the victorie, he hath good knowledge in warres: the theefe will not be found, neyther the thing lost: the accord made betweene two parties will not long continue. In all demaundes which ye may make in this house, this figure is ill, except it be for warre or actes venereall.
