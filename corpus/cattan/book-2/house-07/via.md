If this fygure Via be in this house, the enemy is feeble and of small power, the plaintive shall lose his sute: the theefe is craftie, the fugitive will come no more, for marriage it is ill, for the man will put his wife away, and be separated from her: the gamster shall not winne much, the maide is no virgine. In al the demaunds which ye can put in this house this fygure is ill (except it be for voyages) especially by water.
