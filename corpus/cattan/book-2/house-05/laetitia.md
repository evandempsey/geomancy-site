Finding in this fift house the figure called Letitia, it dooth signifie prosperity, good lucke, and the advauncement of honour to the childe by his good nature, the woman with childe shall have a sonne which shal have great honour and reputation in his life: for al the demaundes which ye can demaunde in this house this figure is good, as to buy and put on newe clothes, and for the plentifulnes and fruitfulnes of the yeere: and consequently this figure is good in al the demaunds even as it is called Lætitia. which is called the house of Joy, wherefore at this time I wil say no other thing, but that it signifyeth to have few children.
