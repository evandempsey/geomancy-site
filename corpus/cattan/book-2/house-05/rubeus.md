In finding in the fift house this figure called Rubeus, it signifyeth perverse children, and degenerate from vertue: it is ill touching eating and drinking, and especially of redde things, the promise shal not be kept, the messenger shall bring letters which shall make mention of warres and combates. And to be briefe this figure hath no signification in this house but ill (excepte to sowe the ground) for the which it is good: the woman wyth childe shall have a daughter, because that this figure is a figure of ♂ R: and moreover, if that there be any children, they shall not live but die quickly.
