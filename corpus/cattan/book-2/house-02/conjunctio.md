Finding in the second house this figure called Conjunctio, ye shall say it signifieth gaine, in things of good industry and spirite, and signifieth, that the thing lost shal be recovered againe, but not without great paine and travel, and long space of time, the company is good and of good spirit, sometime it signifieth bastards, and the obtaining of Bookes and Sciences written by hand, as touching messages, it signifieth quicke arrivall, and also getting of goodes and heretages of folkes lately dead. This figure is commonly good.
