Finding this figure called Fortuna minor in the second house, signifyeth the honour past, great riches and great substance, she is good for merchandize, honors, warres and substance: it signifyeth a readinesse and quicknesse, as well for the way as for all other things, but she is not so good and loyall as that which went last before.
