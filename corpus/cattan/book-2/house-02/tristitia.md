Finding this figure called Tristitia, in the second house it signifyeth losse and hindrance where gaine is hoped, never to attaine unto substance, but by extreame labour, envie, and anger, accompanyed with ill lucke and misfortune, to lose goods, to be spoyled, robbed, overthrowen, and to goe in theeves companie, slowe for journeyes and small profite. This fygure is ill in all things but to fortifie Townes, and make buildings, and signifyeth that the building which ye make shall not be very faire, but it shall last long.
