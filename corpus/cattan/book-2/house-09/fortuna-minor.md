Finding this fygure Fortuna minor in this house, it signifyeth that the man is of great wisedome, doctrine, and knowledge: the man shal have the benefice he pretendes, but it is of no great value, the priest is an honest man, the voyage is long, but the man shall returne home with great profite : the messenger shal quickely returne, the Bookes and Letters make mention of the actes of kinges, princes, and great lordes, the Scholler applieth his learning, and shall come unto honor, the dreame is of kings and emperours : in all things which ye may demaund in this house this fygure is good, but that it declareth a little cholericknesse.
