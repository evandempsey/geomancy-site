Finding this figure Puer in this ninth house, the man shal not have the benefice, but by fine force : in all thinges touching the estate of the Church this fygure is euill: to take a voyage in hand, and for the messenger which bringeth letters, it signifyeth they shall be in daunger to be robbed by the way: the shippe is in great danger to be taken, all that is saide and bruited touching warre is true, the man dreamed of warre or of some redde thing, the Scholler studieth nothing but his weapon, for gaine it is ill, the bookes speake of matters of warre : in all the things which ye may demaund in this house this fygure is euill, but for things touching warre, and the experience of Nigromancie, for the which it is good.
