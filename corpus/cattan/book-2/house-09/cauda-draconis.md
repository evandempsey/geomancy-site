Finding this fygure Cauda draconis in this house, it is ill for the Church, and for the benefice : and for all other things which ye may demaund in this house this fygure is ill (especially for a messenger, and for one which is out of his Country, for they shall be robbed, or assaulted, or pursued by vagabondes, the shippe on the sea shall be robbed by pyrats, or else come home with small gaine.
