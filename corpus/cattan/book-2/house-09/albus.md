If this fygure Albus be founde in this house, it signifyeth great Science, wit, and learning: in all things which ye may demaund: it signifyeth good for the Church, and that the man shall have the benefice : it is good for a Scholler, the dreame is good, it is very good for gaine, the shippe shall returne safely with great substaunce, the man and messenger which be on the way shal returne quickly with joy and blisse, it is good to learne the Arte Oratorie: in all the demaundes which ye may demaund in this house this fygure is good, there is also a messenger by the way which bringeth Letters.
