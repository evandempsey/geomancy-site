Finding this fygure Via in this house, it is ill for the church, the man shall not obtaine the benefice, it is good for a Scholler, the man hath dreamed of drincke or water, or of letters which should be brought unto him: it is ill for gaine, and in all other things, except for long journeys, for in them it signifyeth that the man shall goe safely without any danger or inconvenience, but he shall be long by the way. Besides this, whensoever ye make a fygure for any demaund, and then fynde this figure in this house, there shal a messenger come quickely with letters making mention of a voyage.
