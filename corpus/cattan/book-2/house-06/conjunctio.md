If in the sixt house ye finde this figure Coniunctio, it signifieth that the servantes be meetely good: the sicke person shall die: it is ill to take phisicke, and likewise ill for the Physitian: it is excellent good for whores and baudes: in all other demaundes this figure is ill, but to buy small cattell, whereunto it is good.
