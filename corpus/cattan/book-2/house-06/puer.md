You finding in the sixt house this figure Puer, it signifieth that the servants and subjectes be naught, and will robbe their maisters, it is ill for the sicke person, especially if the first house consent, it is likewise ill for small beastes, for they shall be stolen, the sicke person shall be worse and worse, and fall into worser sicknesse: in all the other questions in this house this figure is ill, but for whores and bawdes, and to rayse men to goe to warre it is good: it is likewise good to take medicine, and especially by drinke.
