If in the sixt house ye finde this figure Leticia, it signifieth the servantes to be good at worke, sure and faithfull: it is good to buy beastes: the patient shall have none other diseases, but shall quickly amende: sometime the sicknesse commeth by over much rest or pensivenesse for some thing: in all other thinges which belong unto this house, this figure is good, except for baudes, shewing that they shall be false, and that their promise made shall take no place.
