Finding in this sixt house this figure Rubeus, it signifyeth no good lucke touching servants, for they shall be in daunger to be robbed or devoured by wolfes: the disease commeth of too much aboundance & corruption of bloud mixed with red choler: the sicke person shall die or be long sicke, if that the first and eight houses doe agree: in all the demaundes which yee may make in this house, this figure is ill, unlesse it be to lett a person bloud.
