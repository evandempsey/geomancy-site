Finding his figure Caput Draconis in the sixt house, it signifieth good luke in beastes, the sicknesse shall last long if the viij. consent thereunto: it is good for the Physitian, and also to take medicine: in all other things which ye may aske in this house, this figures good, but for whores and baudes it is ill, for their matter shal not goe well.
