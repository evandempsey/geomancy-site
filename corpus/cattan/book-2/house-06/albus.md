When in the sixt house ye finde this figure Albus, it signifieth good lucke, as well for servantes as for cattell: the sickenes shall not last long: sometime this figure signifieth good, and sometime ill, according as the Angles do speake: it is good both for the Physitian, and also to take medicine: the disease commeth of phleme: in all other thinges which ye may aske in this house this figure is good, especially for company of servauntes, shewing that they shall be faithfull.
