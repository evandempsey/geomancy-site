When in this house ye finde the figure Via it signifyeth that the servantes will willingly put foorth themselves in their maisters businesse, and doe it with great diligence: it is ill to buye and sell cattell: the sicke person shall be in daunger of death, if the viij. consent thereunto: it is ill for the Phisitian, or to take medicine: the witnesses have falsely deposed: the baude doth but mocke and will not doe her endevour, where she is woorthie to have ten thousand boxes with the fist for her hyre.
