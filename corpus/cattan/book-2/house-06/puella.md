When in this sixt house ye finde this figure Puella, it is very good for all the demaundes which may be made: touching servantes it is ill: for the sicke person and his disease come only by phleme and thought taking for women: for all other thinges which ye may demaunde in this house, this figure is good, especially for whores and bawdes, for their affaires shall prosper: it is also profitable to buy beastes.
