Finding this figure Tristitia in the sixt house, it doth signifie disobedience and naughtinesse in servants and subjects, and that they shall be slothfull and sickly in their service: the sicke person shall die so that the viij. doe consent, the witnesses have falsely deposed: it is ill to buy beastes: for the Phisitian, or to take medicine, & in all things which ye may demand in this house, this figure is ill, but for baudes, for their matters will goe well.
