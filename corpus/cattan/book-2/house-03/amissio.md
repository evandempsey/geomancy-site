Finding this Figure called Amissio in this third house, it signifyeth quarrell and debate with kinsfolkes, in shorte journy it signifyeth speedinesse with small gaine, it is ill for the Scholler, and signifyeth an ill neighbour, and ill company: in all the demaundes which may be made in this house this Figure alwayes signifyeth an ill ende.
