Finding this Figure called Puella in the third house, it signifyeth good and perfect friendship amongest kinsfolks, and profite wyth them, and sheweth also, that they be of good disposition and health, the sute which a person maketh to come unto the favour and love of a woman he can not obtaine it: in shorte voyage it is good, but there will be some tarrying by the way: it is good for a Scholler, but it sheweth that hee is in love with some women in the place where he is resident. In all other things it is good.
