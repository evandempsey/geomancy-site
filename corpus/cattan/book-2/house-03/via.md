When in the third house ye finde this Figure called Via, it signifyeth small good for the kinsfolkes, it sheweth also that the person shall have his desire, his kinsfolkes shall put him in fright by the way, the letters which come from neare at hande bring good newes, it is good for shorte voyage, saving that there will be some delaying in the way, and better to take journey by land then by water, and signifyeth the accomplishement of all desires.
