If ye fynde this Figure called Populus in the third house, it signifyeth amity with kinsfolkes, and that they be many in number, in case of voyage it signifyeth swiftnesse with prosperitie and health, it signifyeth also that embassadors or messengers shall come about the affaires of the people, the Scholler studieth well, and is in a great company of others, the neighbourhoode is great, he that entendeth to take his journey shall finde much company in the way: this Figure is indifferent in all demaundes, and signifyeth rather good then bad.
