When ye find this Figure called Conjunctio in the thirde house, it signifyeth conjunction and amitie amongest kinsfolks, it is good for all things that may be demaunded touching a scholer: it signifyeth also that the kinsfolks be learned, the neighbors good, the thing lost shall be founde againe, the letters which be brought containe none other thing but fables and tales, it is for journey good, and that the messenger is prompt and diligent: in all things this Figure is very good.
