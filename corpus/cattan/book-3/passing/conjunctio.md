This figure *Conjunctio* going from the first to the second, it signifyeth gaine by bookes and writings, in all other houses this figure is indifferent, but in the seventh where it is good for marriage, and in the ninth to put schollers to studie, and in the eight it is ill, for it alwayes signifyeth death, and in the twelfth it is neither good nor badde.
