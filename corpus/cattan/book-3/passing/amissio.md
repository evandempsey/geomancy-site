If you fynde this fygure *Amissio* in the fyrst house, and from thence be found in the seconde house, which we call the going from one house unto an other, it signifyeth losse of goods, and to fall into debates, quarrelles and contentions and sute for goods.

If she passe into the third house, it signifyeth anger and contentions betweene kinsfolks and allyes, it is also ill for small and short journeyes.

If she goe into the fourth house, it signifyeth anger betweene the children and the father, and betweene the father and the children, and by that meanes losse of heritages, and the beginning and sute in lawe the one against the other : and this copulation and assembly of the fygure is very ill but for lecherie.

If she passe into the fift house, the woman with childe shall have unseasonable deliverance : it is also ill to eate and drinke, and signifyeth anger about victuals.

If she passe into the sixt it signifyeth anger against servants, and losse of them and of small cattell.

If she passe thence into the seventh, it signifyeth strifes, quarels and debates with friendes : it is also ill for marriages, and signifyeth to have displeasure with his wife, and to lose the sute.

If she goe into the eight, it signifyeth mortalitie by hoat fevers, and alteration of the understanding, losse of goods of the women, and of the enemie : and in all the demaundes it is ill.

If she passe into the ninth, it signifyeth losse of substance, and to be robbed by the way : it is ill for substance of the Church, and for men learned.

If she goe into the tenth, it signifyeth anger and miscontentment of kings, princes and lordes, and of the sea, and it is ill in all the demaundes of the tenth house.

If shee passe into the eleventh, it signifyeth anger against his friend, and to have an ill issue of the thing pretented or hoped to have.

If she goe into the twelfth, it signifyeth losse of cattell, and imprisonment. And to be short, it is ill in all things.
