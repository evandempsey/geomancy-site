If this figure *Fortuna maior* be in the first house, and thence leape into the second, it signifyeth a great gaine in goods, moveables, as gold, silver, and rich utensilles.

If she passe into the third house, it signifyeth to have much substance by the meanes of the kindred : it is also good for small and short voyages.

If it passe into the fourth, it signifyeth the father to be of good and godly disposition: it is also good to buy heritages and possessions, and a good issue in all things.

If shee goe into the fift, it signifyeth joy, pleasure, to be in good order, well appointed and clothed, to eate and drinke well, and to have fewe children, and they shall be of red colour.

If it passe into the sixt, it signifyeth fidelitie in servantes, and that the sicke person shall amende: it is also good for small beastes.

If shee passe into the seventh it is good for marriage, and signifyeth that the enemie will make peace : for the acquaintance of a Ladie it is not good, because their secretes shall bee disclosed.

If it goe into the eight, it signifyeth death : it is ill in all things but to have the goods of a woman.

If shee passe into the ninth, it signifyeth to contract an amitie with Church men, and men learned, to make a voyage it is good.

If it goe into the tenth, it signifyeth to have dignitie with kings and princes, and with the mother, and to have victorie over his enemies.

If shee passe into the eleventh it signifyeth a good issue upon a good hope, good friendes and succorable.

If it goe into the twelfth it signifyeth imprisonment of the Prince, and that his enemies shall over passe him in might and power.
