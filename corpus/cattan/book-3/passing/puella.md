If this fygure *Puella* goe from the first house to the second, it signifyeth gaine by women, and by white things, in all the other houses where this figure passeth shee is good, especially in the house where she signifyeth joyfulnesse : but in the eight she signifyeth death, and in the twelfth imprisonment.
