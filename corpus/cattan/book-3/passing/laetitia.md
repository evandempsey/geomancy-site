Finding this fygure *Læticia* in the first house, and thence go into the seconde, it signifyeth a mediocritie of gaine by white thinges, and in thinges of the Church.

If it passe into the third, it signifyeth the kinsfolkes to be merry and in good disposition and in health of body, it is also good for small and short voyages.

If she goe into the fourth, the end of all thinges shall be joyfull, but the promise false.

If it passe into the fift, it is good for children, and to eate and drinke, and to sing musicke, and signifyeth a great lightsomnesse of heart.

If she goe into the sixt, it is good for servauntes, signifying that they shall be profitable to their masters: in all the other houses this fygure is good, but in the eight and twelfth, for in the eight she signifyeth death, and in the twelfth losse of cattell. And for because that according to the signification of ech house I have herebefore sufficiently written : you may by the same easely judge ech question that is demaunded, I will not hold you long in the other fygures which followe in touching their mutations and concordances, but onely passe over them generally.
