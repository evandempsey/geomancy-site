If this figure *Fortuna minor*, be in the first house, & thence goe into the second, which we call going from one house to an other, it signifyeth a meane in gaine, and he which medleth with red things shall quickly lose thereby.

If she passe into the third, it signifyeth joy of the kinsfolkes, it is also good for a short voyage, and signifyeth that it shall bee quickly ended, it is also good for friendes.

If it passe into the fourth, it signifyeth that the father is angry or sicke, it also signifyeth the losse of the sute.

If she come into the fift, it signifyeth to have many children which shall be red of colour, also great joy and pleasure, to eate and drinke, and to be costly and well apparelled.

And if she go into the sixt, it signifyeth good to buy small cattle, it is indifferent for servants, and signifyeth that they shall be wittie and wise.

If it passe into the seventh, it signifyeth marriage, and that it shall be with strife and contention, the enemie is strong and mightie, the woman is wise, it is ill in case of love, for it shall be discovered.

If shee goe into the eight, it signifyeth death, and also is ill in all things of esprite, as Magicke and invocations.

If it passe into the ninth, it signifyeth long voyages with all prosperitie and good lucke, and the amitie of men of the Church.

If shee goe into the tenth, it is good and prosperous going to the service of a king, Prince or great Lord, the shippe on the Sea shall come safe and sounde, and the owner thereof shall shortly have newes.

If it passe into the eleventh, it signifieth good and trustie friendes, and a good end of the thing wherein hope is put.

If shee goe into the twelfth, it is ill in all things but to buy horses.
