If you finde this fygure *Albus* in the fyrst house, and be found againe in the second house, it signifyeth gaine and profite in white things, writings or letters, or bookes.

If she passe into the third, it signifyeth good time for friendes and kinsfolkes, and that letters shall come from neare at hand.

If it goe into the fourth, it signifyeth to winne his sute, it is also good in the demaundes which may be made upon the father, signifying as well in them as in all others a good issue and ende.

If she passe into the fift, it signifyeth to have many children, and to delight in white clothes, to eate and drinke, and to company often times with learned men.

If it passe into the sixt the servants be good and trustie.

If she goe into the seventh the marriage lately begunne shall take effect to the great contentment and profite of the parties, and the enemies shall demaund peace.

And if it passe into the eight, it signifyeth death by a hoat disease of rumes and cathers, it is also good for magicke.

If she goe into the ninth, it signifyeth that the long journey shalbe good and profitable, and that the letters which come from a farre off bring good newes, it is also good for Doctors & churchmen, and to make amitie and acquaintance with them.

If it passe into the tenth it is good to goe to Kings, Princes and Lordes, and to goe to visite his mother.

If shee goe into the eleventh it bringeth good lucke, and also for letters which shall come on their part.

If it passe into the twelfth, it is good to buye frames, also in this place it is a token to be held prisoner.
