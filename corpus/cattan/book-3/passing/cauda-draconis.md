If this figure *Cauda draconis* be found in the first house, then the figure should not be judged, but it must be broken and an other made one houre after that : but if from the seconde house she goe into any of the other ye may there judge it, saving for the fourth, where there is no certaine judgement to be given, for the malice of the sayde fygure and therefore at this time I will say no more.
