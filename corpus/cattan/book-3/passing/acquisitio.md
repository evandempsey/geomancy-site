IF this figure named *Aquisitio*, do in mounting growe from the first house into the seconde, that is to say, that he be in the second or be like unto the first where *Aquisitio* is, it signifyeth to gaine gownes, and clothing apparell, to get honour, and to make gaine and profit in all acts of honour and vertue, and with the like people.

If that from the first she goe into the third, it signifyeth to have honour, joy, and profite of the kinsefolks and friends.

If that shee goe from the first to the fourth, it signifyeth profite by the father, and a good and joyfull end of his affaires.

If that shee goe from the first to the fyft, it signifyeth joy of children, that is to say, they shall be good, and of honour, and to have joy and pleasure, to eate and drinke, and to bee well clothed.

If that she goe from the first to the sixt, it signifyeth profite and good lucke for servants, and to buy small cattell.

If she goe into the seventh, it signifyeth to make a contract of enemies, to make marriages, and to get honour and the love of persons, and for all demaundes it is good.

If she goe from the first into the eight, it signifyeth death for the thing demaunded, or for the demaunder and great profite in succession and heritages : it is also good for thinges of magicke.

If she goe from the first to the ninth, it signifyeth to have profite and gaine by voyages, and in things of the Church, as in benefices or offices : it is also good for the profite of the children that yee would put to schole, for they shall have promotions Ecclesiasticall, and they shall be men of knowledge.

If she passe from the fyrst to the tenth house, it signifyeth amitie, familiaritie, acquaintance and profite with kings, princes, and great lordes: likewise to receive honour and dignitie by the mother : and also profite in succession and heritages. And to be briefe, it is good in all things.

If she goe from the fyrst to the eleventh, it signifyeth to have honour and profite by his friendes, and a good hope of the question demaunded.

If she passe from the fyrst to the twelfth, it signifyeth imprisonment, losse of beastes and to be overcome by the enemie: and to be briefe, it is ill in all thinges.
