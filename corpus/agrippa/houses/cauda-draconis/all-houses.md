*Cauda Draconis,* in all and singular the respective houses aforesaid, giveth the contrary judgment to *Caput.* And these are the natures of the figures of Geomancy and their judgments, in all and singular their houses, upon all manner of questions to be propounded, of, or concerning any matter or thing whatsoever.
