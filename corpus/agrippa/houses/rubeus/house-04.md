In the fourth house he destroyeth and consumeth partimonies, and disperseth and wasteth inheritances, causeth them to come to nothing; destroyeth the fruits of the Field by tempestuous seasons, and malignancy of the earth; and bringeth the father to a sudden death. *Rubeus*
