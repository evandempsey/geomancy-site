In the Fifth house he giveth obedient children, endued with good manners, and in whom shall be had the greatest joy and comfort of old age; signifies a woman with child to bring forth a daughter; sheweth honourable embassages,

and declares rumours and news to be altogether true, and leaveth good and ample fame after death.
