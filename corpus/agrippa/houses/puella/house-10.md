In the tenth house *Puella* signifies princes not to be very potent, but notwithstanding they shall govern peaceably within their dominions, and shall be beloved of their neighbours and subjects; it causeth them to be affable, mild and courteous, and that they shall always exercise themselves with continual mirth, plays and huntings; maket• judges to be good, godly and merciful; giveth offices about women, or especially from noble women.
