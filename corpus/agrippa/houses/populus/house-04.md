In the fourth house, it signifies a father to be sickly, and of a laborious life, and his earthly possessions and inheritane to be taken away: sheweth profit to be gained by water: sheweth treasure not to be hid; or if there be any hidden, that it shall not be found: a patrimony to be preserved with great labour.
