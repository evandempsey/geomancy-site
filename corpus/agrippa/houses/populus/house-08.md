In the eight house, it denotes sudden death without any long sickness or anguish, and oftentimes sheweth death by the water; giveth no inheritance, possession or legacies from the dead; and if any be, they shall be lost by some intervening contentions, or other discord: he signifieth the dowry of a wife to be little or none. *Populus*
