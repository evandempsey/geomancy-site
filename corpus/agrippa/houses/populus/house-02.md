In the second house, *Populus* sheweth a mean substance, and that to be gotten with great difficulty: maketh a man also always sensible of laborious toil: things stolen are never regained: what is lost shall never be wholly recovered:

that which is hidden shall not be found. But if the question be of a thief, it declareth him not yet to be fled away, but to lie lurking within the City.
