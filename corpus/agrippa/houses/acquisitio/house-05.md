In the Fi•th house, *Acquisitio* signifies many children of both sexes, but more males than females; sheweth a woman to be with child, and that she shall be delivered without danger• and if a question be propounded concerning an• sex, he signi•es it to be masculine: encreaseth gainful profitable embassages and messages, but

extendeth fame not far after death, yet causeth a man to be inherited of his own, and signifieth rumours to be true.
