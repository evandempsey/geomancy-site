In the third house, many brethren and they to be wealthy; many gainful journeys; signi•es a man of good faith. In the fourth is signified a patrimony of much riches, many pos•essions of copious fruits; he signi•jeth that treasure hid in any place shall be found and sheweth a f•ther to be very rich, but covetous.
