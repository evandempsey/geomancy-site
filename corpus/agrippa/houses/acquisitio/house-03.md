In the third house, many brethren and they to be wealthy; many gainful journeys; signifies a man of good faith. In the fourth is signified a patrimony of much riches, many possessions of copious fruits; he signifieth that treasure hid in any place shall be found and sheweth a father to be very rich, but covetous.
