*Acquisitio* found in the first house, giveth a long life and prosperous old age; signifieth a man of a middle stature, and a great head, a countenance very well to be distinguished or known a long nose, much beard, hair curling, and fair eyes; free of his meat and drink, but in all things else sparing, and not liberal.
