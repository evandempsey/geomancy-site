in the ninth house, causeth long journeys by water, especially by sea, and portendeth very great gains to be acquired thereby; he denoteth

Priesthoods, and profits from Ecclesiastical employments: maketh men of good religion, upright, and constant faith: sheweth dreams to be true, whose signification shall suddenly appear: increaseth Philosophical and Grammatical sciences, and those things which appertain to the instruction and bringing up of children.
