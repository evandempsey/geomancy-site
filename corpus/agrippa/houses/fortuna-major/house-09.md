In the ninth house, he signifieth journeys to be prosperous; and by land on horseback, rather th•n on foot; to be long, and not soon accomplished: he sheweth the return of those that are absent; signifies men to be of

good faith, and constant in their intentions; and religious; and that never change or alter their faith: Dreams he presageth to be true; signifieth true and perfect sciences.
