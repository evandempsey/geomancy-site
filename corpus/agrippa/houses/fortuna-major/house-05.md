In the fifth house, he giveth

by children, and causeth them to attain to great honours: Embassages he rendereth prosperous; but they are purchased with pains and prayers: he noteth rumours to be true: he bestoweth public honours, and causeth a man to be very famous after death: foresheweth a woman with child to bring forth a man child.
