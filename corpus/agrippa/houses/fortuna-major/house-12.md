In the twelfth house, if a question be proposed of the quality of enemies, it demonstrateth them to be potent and noble, and hardly to be resisted; But if a question shall be concerning any other condition or respect of the enemies, he will deliver from their treacheries. It signifies faithful servants; reduceth fugitives; hath signification of animals, as horses, lions, and bulls: freeth from imprisonments; and eminent dangers he either mitigateth or taketh away.
