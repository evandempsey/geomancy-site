In the seventh house, he giveth a wife of a good progeny descended; but you shall be incumbred with many

troubles with her: causeth love to be anxious and unconstant: prolongeth contentions, and maketh ones adversaries to circumvent him with many cavillations; but in process of time he giveth victory.
