In the tenth house *Carcer* causeth Princes to be very wicked, and wretchedly to perish, because when they are established in their power, they will wholly adict themselves to every voluptuous lust, pleasure and tyranny; causeth Judges to be unjust and false; declareth the mother to be cruel, and infamous, and noted with the badge of adultery; giveth no Offices nor Magistracies, but such as are gotten and obtained either by lying, or through theft, and base and cruel robbery.
