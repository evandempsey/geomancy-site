In the eighth house *Carcer* declareth the kind of death to be by some fall, mischance, or false accusation, or that men shall be condemned in prison, or in public judgment, and sheweth them to be put to death, or that they shall often lay violent and deadly hands upon themselves; denieth a wife to have any portion or legacies. *Carcer*
