In the fourth

house *Tristitia* consumeth and destroyeth fields, possessions and inheritances; causeth a father to be old and of long life, and a very covetous hoarder up of money. In the fifth hous• it signifies no children, or that they shall soon die, sheweth a woman with child to bring forth a woman child, giveth no fame nor honours.
