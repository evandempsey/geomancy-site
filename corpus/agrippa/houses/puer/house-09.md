In the ninth house *puer* sheweth journeys not to be undergone without peril and danger of life, yet nevertheless declareth them to be accomplished prosperously and safely; sheweth persons of little religion▪ and using little conscience; notwithstanding giveth the knowledge of natural philosophy and physic, and many other liberal and excellent arts. *Puer*
