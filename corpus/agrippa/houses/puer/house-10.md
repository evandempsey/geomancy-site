in the tenth house signifies princes to be powerful, glorious, and famous in warlike atchievments; but they

shall be unconstant and changeable, by reason of the mutable and various success of victory. *Puer* in this house causeth judges to be cruel and unmerciful; increaseth offices in warlike affairs; signifies magistracy to be exercised by Fire and sword; hurteth a mother, and endangereth her life.
