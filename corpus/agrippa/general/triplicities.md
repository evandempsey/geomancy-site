But if any will constitute these triplicities according to the natures of the planets, and figures themselves, let him observe this rule, that *Fortuna major, Rubeus, Puer,* and *Amissio,* do make the fiery triplicity: *Fortuna minor, Puella, Laetitia,* and *Conjunctio,* the triplicity of the air: *Acquisitio,* the Dragons tail, *Via,* and *Populus,* do govern the watry triplicity; and the earthly triplicity is ruled by *Carcer, Tristitia, Albus,* and the Dragons head.
